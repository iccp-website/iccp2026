Important Notes

For papers accepted to ICCP 2026, at least one author per paper must be registered. Please visit iccp2026.iccp-conference.org for additional details.

Early registration ends June 11, 2026 at 11:59 PM ET.

Refund Policy

All refund requests must be submitted in writing to iccp2026finance@gmail.com by the early registration deadline of June 11, 2026 at 11:59 PM ET. No refunds will be issued after that date. All refunds are subject to a $50 USD cancellation fee.

We are happy to change the name on an existing registration for no additional fee, provided the change is within the same category (e.g., IEEE student member to another IEEE student member).

Visa Letters

We only issue visa invitation letters to registered attendees. To request a letter, complete your registration and forward your confirmation email to iccp2026finance@gmail.com indicating that you require an invitation letter.

If your visa is denied and you are unable to attend in person, please contact us—we will issue a full refund of the registration fee.

Questions

For any questions, please contact us at iccp2026finance@gmail.com.
