Conference and Banquet Venues

Conference Venue

The conference will take place in the Frick Chemistry Laboratory, with lectures held in the Frick B02 auditorium.

Opening Night Reception: The opening reception will be held in Taylor Atrium, located on the first floor of Frick Chemistry Lab. The atrium provides a welcoming space for attendees to gather and network on the evening of July 13th.

Getting to Frick Chemistry Lab from The Nassau Inn: Walk east on Nassau Street and turn right onto Washington Road. Follow Washington Road south for approximately 10 minutes. Frick Chemistry Laboratory will be on your left at the corner of Washington Road and Scholar Way. The walk is approximately 15 minutes. Alternatively, take the TigerTransit campus shuttle from Palmer Square to the Natural Sciences Neighborhood.

Banquet Venue

The banquet will take place in the Princeton University Art Museum on the evening of July 14th.

Getting to Princeton Art Museum from The Nassau Inn: Exit the hotel onto Palmer Square and walk east toward Nassau Street. Cross Nassau Street and enter Princeton University campus through FitzRandolph Gate. Continue straight along the main path for approximately 5 minutes. The Art Museum will be on your left, a distinctive modern building with stone facades. The walk is approximately 8-10 minutes.

Getting to Princeton Art Museum from Frick Chemistry Lab: Walk north on Washington Road toward Nassau Street. Turn left onto Nassau Street and enter campus through FitzRandolph Gate. Follow the main path to the Art Museum. The walk is approximately 12-15 minutes. Alternatively, walk through campus via the main pathways for a more scenic route of similar duration.
