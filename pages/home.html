Welcome to the official website of ICCP 2026—the premier annual conference on all topics related to computational imaging. The conference brings together researchers with interests broadly related to advancing computational imaging and computational photography, from theory to systems to applications, including sensors, optics, algorithms, machine intelligence, vision science and perception.

ICCP 2026 will be an in-person event at the Princeton University in Princeton, NJ, USA, from July 13–15, 2026. Additional information will be posted here as we get closer to the event.
