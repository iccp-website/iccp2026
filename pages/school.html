Summer School on Computational Imaging

The second ICCP Summer School on Computational Imaging will take place July 11–12, 2026, immediately before the ICCP 2026 main conference, at Princeton University. There will be registration options available for undergraduate and graduate students, as well as non-student researchers from academia and industry (new this year). As in 2025, the school will emphasize hands-on courses on current research topics in computational imaging and computational photography.

We will update this page in early 2026 to provide more information. For any questions, please contact the Summer School Chairs (Katie Bouman and Ioannis Gkioulekas) at iccp2026school@gmail.com.
